Film Projects

Videography projects for a number of clients

Moneyreagh Primary School - Freedom for Girls

As part of Moneyreagh's Erasmus+ project pupils learned about their rights as listed by the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child. After watching a video by Global Goals, Yr 7 decided to make their own version. The girls worked hard to learn lyrics and perfect their fiercest expressions. Filmed on Fuji XT3. Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro. Text animation in Adobe After Effects

Rathmore Primary School - Great Escape

“In 2018, there was an attempted mass escape from Rathmore PS. Will these pupils be successful in gaining their freedom?”

Video for Rathmore Primary School 2018 leavers' assembly. Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro with some compositing in After Effects.

Hamilton Road Presbyterian Church - Narnia Battle

As part of HRPC's 'Narnia experience' in 2017 we filmed a short battle sequence in Crawfordsburn Forest Park. This involved extensive pre-production, including scriptwriting, storyboarding and shotlisting, two days filming on location with a cast of 40, and post production work with Premiere Pro and After Effects.
